About

An institutional approach
to UAE real estate.

Duneshore Capital is a real estate private equity business being established in Dubai, UAE. We identify, structure, and manage real estate investments in UAE freehold zones — partnering with strong operators and platforms to generate institutional-quality returns.

Our approach is rooted in rigorous investment discipline, institutional governance, and genuine alignment with our investors. We are building something designed to stand the test of time.

We are currently in formation. Details of our investment strategy and fund structure are available on request to qualified parties.

Approach

How we invest.

01

Freehold Assets in UAE Growth Markets

We focus exclusively on freehold zones — areas where corporate entities hold full ownership rights. This is a fundamental requirement for institutional fund structures and provides the clearest path to capital appreciation and exit.

02

Backing Strong Operators & Platforms

We partner with proven real estate operators, JV partners, and platforms — providing institutional capital, fund structure, and governance alongside their execution capability. We back people, not just assets.

03

Institutional Governance from Day One

Every investment is structured with institutional-grade documentation, governance, and reporting. We operate under a regulated fund management platform, providing investors with the protections and oversight they should expect.

Founder

Neil Sarkhel

Neil Sarkhel is a real estate fund manager, capital raiser, and business builder with 16 years of institutional real estate private equity experience.

Over ten years at Newcore Capital, he helped build a UK real estate fund management business to $800m of assets under management — structuring seven institutional funds, raising $600m+ of capital, and managing 120+ investor relationships across pension funds, insurance companies, family offices, and high-net-worth individuals. He exited his ownership stake in August 2025.

Neil holds a Masters in Real Estate Finance from the University of Cambridge, attended the Oxford Real Estate Programme at Saïd Business School, and began his career at Goldman Sachs in London. He is based in Dubai.
